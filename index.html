1. Chop poatoes into quarters
  2. Simmer salted water for 15 - 20 minutes until tender
  3. Heat milk, butter and nutmeg
  4. Drain potetoes and mash
  5. Mix in the milk mixture
  • 1kg King Edward potetoes
  • 100ml milk
  • 50g salted butter
  • Freshly grated nutmeg
  • salt and papper to teste
